1

Register a free PayFast account

Visit payfast.io → Sign Up → choose Business → enter your SA bank details. No monthly fee, you only pay when you make sales.

In your PayFast dashboard go to Settings → Integration. Copy both your Merchant ID and Merchant Key.

In PayFast dashboard go to "Split Payments" and enable it. This automatically splits each sale, 90% to the author, 10% to you, the moment payment is made.

Where This Began

The silence that follows publishing your first book

You spend months, sometimes years, pouring yourself into a book. You publish it, you share it, and then you wait. And often, the silence is deafening. Not because your work isn't good enough. But because the platforms that exist weren't built with you in mind.

Independent authors, especially new voices, are left to figure out discovery, promotion, and sales entirely on their own. You follow bigger pages hoping for a feature. You submit your book and hear nothing back. You watch others get spotlights you never received, and you start to wonder if your story even matters.

It does. And that's exactly why The Ink Uprising exists.

Our Mission

A shelf that sees every writer

The Ink Uprising is a marketplace built specifically for independent authors, journals, eBooks, poetry collections, fiction, non-fiction, and everything in between. We do not gatekeep based on follower count, publishing credentials, or how well-connected you are.

If you've written something with intention and heart, there is a place for it here. We review every submission personally, and once approved, your book receives real promotional support, including AI-generated social media posts crafted specifically for your work, shared to our audience.

How to get your book listed

1

Complete the submission form

Head to "Sell Your Book" and fill in your book title, description, price, type, and your PayFast email. This is where your 90% will be sent each time someone buys your book.

2

Upload your PDF

Your book file should be a PDF, formatted, proofread, and ready for readers. It doesn't have to be perfect, but it should reflect your best work. Max file size is 50MB.

3

We review your submission

Every submission is personally reviewed. We're looking for work submitted with intention, not perfection. We'll confirm by WhatsApp or email once your book is approved and live on the shelf.

4

Your book gets promoted

Once approved, we create a promotional social media post for your book and share it. Not just a listing, actual promotion. Because that's what we wish someone had done for us.

5

Earn automatically on every sale

When a reader buys your book, PayFast's Split Payment system automatically sends 90% to your PayFast account in real time. No waiting, no manual transfers, no chasing anyone.
